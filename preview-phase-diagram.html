↗ Top-Left: Early Stress Delinquencies rising but banks haven't tightened yet. Early warning — stress building beneath the surface.
↗ Top-Right: Crisis High tightening + high delinquency = credit crunch. Banks pulling back as losses mount. (2008-09, 2020)
↙ Bottom-Right: Recovery Banks still cautious (high tightening) but delinquencies improving. Credit cycle healing.
